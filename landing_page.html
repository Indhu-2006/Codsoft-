About Us

Pop & Spark – Lighting Up Every Celebration!

At Pop & Spark, we believe every festival, party, and special occasion deserves a little extra sparkle. Since our founding, we’ve been committed to bringing joy, color, and excitement to your celebrations with high-quality, safe, and vibrant fireworks.

Our collection includes sparklers, rockets, flower pots, ground chakkars, and more — perfect for creating unforgettable memories with family and friends. Safety and fun go hand-in-hand for us, which is why all our products meet strict quality standards.

Whether it’s Diwali, New Year, or a special event, Pop & Spark is your trusted partner in making every moment bright, colorful, and full of happiness.

Our Mission: To bring smiles to every celebration with safe, dazzling, and unforgettable fireworks.

Our Vision: A world where every festival sparkles, and joy is shared with everyone.
